Complete Printer Setup & Training for Green & Co. Advocates

ProPrint Solutions provided a full printer installation and maintenance package for a leading law firm in Nairobi. The project included the delivery and setup of HP LaserJet multifunction printers, wireless configuration, and integration with the client’s office network.

We also trained the staff on efficient usage and troubleshooting techniques, ensuring smooth daily operations. Our maintenance plan now supports the client with monthly inspections and same-day repair response when needed.

  • Installed 3 high-capacity HP LaserJet printers.
  • Configured secure wireless printing for 20+ users.
  • Provided toner refilling and recycling service.
  • Implemented preventive maintenance schedule.

The successful implementation helped the client reduce downtime by 60% and printing costs by 35%, proving that professional printer servicing brings both reliability and long-term savings.
